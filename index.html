Why Columbia, SC Sucks

Let’s be honest — not every city is perfect. Here are a few reasons why Columbia, South Carolina might not be everyone's cup of sweet tea:

  • It's Hot. Like, Really Hot.
    If you enjoy sweating through your shirt before 9 AM, you're in luck! Columbia turns into an oven every summer — and spring... and fall.
  • The Traffic Makes No Sense.
    Want to sit at a red light with no other cars around? Columbia has you covered.
  • Everything Closes Early.
    Planning a late-night snack run? Sorry — Columbia's probably already asleep.
  • It Floods. A Lot.
    Rainy day? You might need a canoe. Or maybe just build an ark.
  • The Smell Near the Riverfront.
    You’ll know it when you smell it. Trust us.
  • The State House Vibes.
    The political energy downtown is... tense, to say the least.
  • Where’s the Fun?
    Sure, there are a few bars and a zoo, but the excitement level is closer to “Sunday afternoon nap.”

Of course, if you're into brutal heat, political drama, and limited nightlife, Columbia might just be your paradise!
